Proper Form Guidelines

Beginner Level

Foundation Techniques

  • Posture: Maintain neutral spine alignment
  • Breathing: Exhale during exertion
  • Range of Motion: Gradually increase
  • Core Engagement: Gentle abdominal activation
  • Joint Alignment: Knees tracking over toes
  • Tempo Control: Slow, deliberate movements

Intermediate Level

Advanced Techniques

  • Bracing: Proper abdominal bracing
  • Scapular Control: Shoulder blade stability
  • Hip Hinge: Correct deadlift hinge
  • Knee Tracking: Maintain alignment
  • Grip Strength: Strong pulling techniques
  • Progressive Loading: 5‚Äì10% increases

Advanced Level

Elite Techniques

  • Triple Extension: Full hip/knee/ankle extension
  • Eccentric Control: Slow negatives
  • Depth Precision: Safe deep squats
  • Stability Under Load: 80‚Äì90% max weight
  • Landing Mechanics: Soft plyometric landings
  • Movement Velocity: Controlled tempo

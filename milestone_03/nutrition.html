Our Nutrition Principles

Simple, sustainable habits that work. No fad diets or banned foods — just smart strategies.

01

Protein First

Hit your daily protein target (0.8–1g per lb bodyweight) to stay full, preserve muscle, and recover better.

02

Portion Control

Use simple hand-size portions — no calorie counting needed. Adjust portions based on your goals.

03

Flexible Foods

No banned foods. Build meals you enjoy while hitting your nutrition targets.

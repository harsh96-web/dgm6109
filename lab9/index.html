About This Project

This project explores the relationships between workout duration, sleep quality, protein intake, and overall restfulness through two different scatterplot visualizations of my 12-day fitness tracking data (September 25 – October 6, 2025).

Enhanced from Lab 8 based on professor feedback to include specific exercise details (bench press weights, squat/deadlift loads, cardio types) in interactive tooltips rather than just duration.

Each plot uses D3.js v7.6.1 and incorporates both Array.filter() and Array.sort() methods to manipulate and display the data effectively.

Array Methods Used:

  • Plot 1 — Array.sort(): Sorts data chronologically by date to show my training progression over 12 days (Sept 25 – Oct 6), with color showing restfulness improvement.
  • Plot 2 — Array.filter(): Filters to show only adequate-sleep nights (more than 6 hours) to analyze how workout intensity affects rest quality, with color showing time progression.

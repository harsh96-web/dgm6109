Lab #9 — AI Deep-Think Recovery Scatterplots

Harsh Kumar

This project investigates how workout quality and recovery interact, using my personal fitness dataset combined with AI-assisted pattern recognition. Each record includes workout duration, type/intensity (e.g., heavy lifts vs cardio), sleep hours, protein intake, and restfulness. The goal is to uncover data-driven recovery patterns that could later train an AI model to predict fatigue or performance readiness.

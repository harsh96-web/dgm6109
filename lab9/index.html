About This Project

This project explores the relationships between workout duration, sleep quality, protein intake, and overall restfulness through two scatterplot visualizations of my 12-day fitness tracking data (September 25 – October 6, 2025).

It builds upon my Lab 8 dataset and includes detailed workout information (bench, squat, deadlift, cardio) shown in interactive tooltips to better connect training intensity with recovery, performance, and daily well-being.

Each plot uses D3.js v7.6.1 and incorporates Array.filter() and Array.sort() to prepare and display the data effectively.

Array Methods Used

  • Plot 1 — Array.sort(): Sorts data chronologically to show training progression across Sept 25–Oct 6; color encodes restfulness improvement.
  • Plot 2 — Array.filter(): Filters nights with sleep > 6 hours to analyze how workout intensity affects rest quality; color shows time progression.
